The Project ☟

We got married!

Yay! Being a graphic designer in a previous life, I wanted to do all of my branding and design for the wedding, including coding a simple static website.

The Inspo ☟

Moody botanicals.

We got engaged at the local conservatory which was flush with colorful tropical plant life. We pulled from the dark maroons and greens of the bromeliads and the organic botanical shapes Matisse liked to create.

The Branding ☟

The finest free and open source fonts.

I found two gorgeous fonts from velvetyne.fr. The fonts Le Murmure By Jérémy Landes and Sporting Grotesque By Lucas Le Bihan+ George Triantafyllakos+ Maciej Połczyński stole the show as our 2 display fonts.
