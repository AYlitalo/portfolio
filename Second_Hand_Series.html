Client ☟

Mile High WorkShop is a job training facility in Aurora, Colorado that creates jobs while working with big brands to make cool products.

Ad2Colorado chose Mile High Workshop as their 2019/2020 creative partner for the Ad2 National Public Service Project. 

Project ☟

Second Chance Series

The goal defined by the strategy team was to develop a sub brand for the client focusing on direct-to-consumer products instead of their usual wholesale business model. The product they’d begin trialing would be unique and one-of-a-kind tote bags made from upcycled graphic heavy billboards from different brands both local and national.  The work provided by Ad2Colorado included branding and naming, strategy, photography, social media and billboard advertising, email campaigns, and website/e-commerce store design and development.

I was responsible for designing the website/e-commerce store.

Wireframes

The common conventions/patterns for e-commerce shops are pretty well defined, so it didn’t take too much time testing and iterating. I spent some more time exploring different UI layouts finding ways to display information in a fun and still digestable manner.

We knew we were going to develop in Wordpress, so designing something that would be easily created from a template was also important.
